PayPal Login Problems: Common Causes and Safe Fixes

If you cannot log in to PayPal, treat it as both a technical issue and a security issue. Use official recovery only.

Short answer

Start with the official PayPal sign-in page or app. Check your password, two-factor authentication, phone number, email access, app version, browser cookies, VPN, and whether PayPal has limited the account. Do not trust social media support accounts or anyone asking for a one-time code. If you lost access to your phone number or email, use PayPal’s official recovery and help pages.

Main Steps

1. Use the official PayPal login page

Type paypal.com directly or open the official PayPal app. Avoid clicking login links from emails, ads, or social posts if you are unsure.

2. Reset your password carefully

If the password fails, use PayPal’s official password reset. Make sure your email account is secure before resetting financial accounts.

3. Check two-factor authentication

If your code is not arriving, check signal, authenticator app time sync, and whether you changed phone numbers. Never share a 2FA code with anyone.

4. Try another browser or update the app

Browser cookies, extensions, VPNs, and old app versions can block login. Try a clean browser session or update the app from the official store.

5. Look for account limitation messages

If PayPal limits the account, follow the instructions inside your PayPal account or official help. Do not upload documents through third-party links.

6. Contact official support only

PayPal support scams are common. Social media replies, phone numbers in search ads, and chat accounts can be fake.

Safety Notes

Use official channels and protect verification codes. PayPal is a financial account. Never share passwords, one-time codes, card details, or identity documents through unofficial links. If an email says your account is locked, go to PayPal directly instead of clicking the link.

This is general account-safety information, not financial advice. If a page, message, ad, or support account asks you to share one-time codes, passwords, payment details, or remote access, stop and verify through the official app or official help page.

FAQ

Why cannot I log in to PayPal?

Common causes include password errors, 2FA problems, browser issues, app updates, VPNs, account limitations, or security checks.

What if I lost access to my phone number?

Use PayPal’s official recovery options. Do not ask strangers or third-party services to receive codes.

Is PayPal account recovery safe?

It is safest when started from paypal.com or the official app. Avoid recovery links from unknown messages.

How do I avoid PayPal phishing?

Type paypal.com directly, check sender details, avoid urgent links, and never share one-time codes.

Should I contact PayPal support?

Yes, if official recovery does not solve it. Use only official PayPal support channels.
