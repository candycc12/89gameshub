Start with the official NFL schedule. Do not assume the same network carries the same window every week. National windows and special games can move your viewing path.

Some NFL games are easy to find across national windows. Others depend more on your local market and station availability. This is why local channel access matters so much for NFL fans.

3. Match your device to the right app
