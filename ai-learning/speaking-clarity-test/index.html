FAQ

Questions before you start

Is this for adults or kids?

Both can use the structure. Adults can use work prompts, and kids can use school or story prompts.

Do I need to record my voice?

The full experience works best with speaking, but you can start by choosing and rewriting answers.

What is the first result?

You get a recommended path based on your biggest speaking gap.

Can this help with social confidence?

It can help you prepare clearer answers, which often makes speaking feel less stressful.
