World Cup Predictor FAQ

Are these predictions betting advice?

No. This is entertainment and fan discussion content only.

How are predictions made?

The test version uses placeholder match data. The formal version should insert verified fixtures, team form, player availability, rankings, and recent results.

Can I share my prediction?

Yes. The quiz result includes share-style copy for friends and group chats.

Do predictions update after the match?

The page structure supports post-match updates, including final score, recap, and prediction result.

What is the Match Oracle?

A playful fan predictor that gives a fun pick, confidence level, and one data-based reason.

Can I see group stage predictions?

The test version reserves this as a future page type if the first pages perform well.

Can fans vote?

Yes. Poll modules are included for winner picks and fan sentiment testing.

Why are some fixtures placeholders?

Because the test version avoids publishing unverified live schedule data. Formal pages should be checked against reliable sources before release.
