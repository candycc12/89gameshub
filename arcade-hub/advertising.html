Advertising Disclosure

说明本站广告、赞助内容和第三方跳转链接的展示方式。

广告说明

Arcade Hub 可能通过广告、赞助位、应用推广、联盟链接或其他商业合作获得收入。

广告标识

我们会尽量使用 “Advertisement”、“Ad”、“Sponsored” 或类似标识区分广告内容和普通游戏内容。

广告点击与第三方页面

点击广告后，你可能会离开 Arcade Hub 并访问第三方网站、应用商店或广告主页面。第三方页面的内容、下载、付款、隐私和服务条款由对应第三方负责。

游戏前广告

部分游戏启动前可能展示广告或赞助信息。我们会尽量清楚展示倒计时、继续游戏提示和广告标识，避免将广告伪装成游戏操作。

合作联系

广告投放、品牌合作或游戏推广请联系：appvalue@outlook.com

Advertising Disclosure

Arcade Hub may earn revenue through advertisements, sponsored placements, app promotions, affiliate links, or other commercial partnerships.

Ad labeling

We aim to distinguish advertising from regular game content by using labels such as “Advertisement,” “Ad,” “Sponsored,” or similar wording.

Ad clicks and third-party pages

When you click an ad, you may leave Arcade Hub and visit a third-party website, app store, or advertiser page. Third-party content, downloads, payments, privacy practices, and terms are controlled by the respective third party.

Pre-game ads

Some game sessions may show advertising or sponsored information before the game starts. We aim to show countdowns, continue-playing messages, and ad labels clearly, without disguising ads as game controls.

Partnerships

For advertising, brand partnerships, or game promotion, contact: appvalue@outlook.com
